The Competitive Landscape

The Turkish low-voltage market is dominated by established global players who have solidified their positions through extensive distributor networks. Understanding their footprint is key to carving out Socomec's niche as a premium European alternative.

The Digital Battleground: Where Professionals Engage

Social media in Turkey is not just for consumers; it's a vital B2B tool for building trust and credibility. A multi-platform approach is essential to connect with decision-makers and technical professionals where they are most active.

Socomec's Phased E-Commerce Rollout

A strategic, phased approach to e-commerce will maximize impact while minimizing channel conflict. The plan prioritizes empowering distributor partners first, building a strong foundation before expanding to broader consumer-facing marketplaces.

Phase 1: Distributor-Led E-Commerce (Months 0-6)

Empower 2-3 key distributors with a complete digital package to leverage their existing platforms and customer base for rapid market penetration.

Phase 2: Launch Socomec B2B Portal (Months 6-12)

Introduce a dedicated, password-protected portal for partners, providing real-time inventory, ordering, and exclusive marketing assets to strengthen the ecosystem.

Phase 3: Selective B2C Marketplace Presence (Month 12+)

Strategically list curated products on platforms like Hepsiburada and Trendyol to build broad brand awareness and capture the "prosumer" market segment.
